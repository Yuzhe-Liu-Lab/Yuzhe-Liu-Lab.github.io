        Resources from the Yuzhe Liu Lab include open-source computational tools and research devices for head-impact biomechanics.

        ## [Instrumented Mouthguard](instrumented-mouthguard/)

        A custom-fit smart mouthguard for precise six-axis head-impact kinematics measurement, wireless data transfer, and wireless charging. 智能牙套用于精确测量头部碰撞六轴运动，并支持蓝牙传输和无线充电。

        ## [Deep Learning Brain Model](deep-learning-brain-model/)

        A deep learning model for estimating spatiotemporal brain-strain distributions from six-axis head kinematics measured by wearable devices. 深度学习大脑模型可根据可穿戴设备采集的头部六轴运动计算大脑应变时空分布。

        ## [Brain-RBF](brain-rbf/)

        A radial-basis-function workflow for morphing baseline finite element head models to target brain morphologies.

        ## [FE Head Impact Simulations](fe-head-impact-simulations/)

        MATLAB-based resources for finite element head-impact simulation and stress/strain analysis.

        ## [Clustering Template for Brain Strain](clustering-template-brain-strain/)

        Templates and analysis resources for organizing and clustering brain-strain simulation outputs.
